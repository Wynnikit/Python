Applicants are required to write a 600 word maximum personal statement describing 
their interest in IT Networking. The essay should help to answer the questions: 

Why do you want to complete this degree? What are your aspirations and goals? 
What unique qualifications make you likely to succeed in this program? 
What experiences contribute to your awareness and skills required for this field? 

Please follow the instructions carefully. Be as thorough as possible within the 
specified essay guidelines.
